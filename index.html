About Our Shop

Coffee distribution

At Coffo, we're passionate about bringing you the finest coffee experience. Founded in 2010, we've dedicated ourselves to sourcing the highest quality beans from sustainable farms around the world. Our expert roasters carefully craft each batch to highlight the unique characteristics of every bean.

Our cozy shop is designed to be your sanctuary in the city - a place where you can enjoy exceptional coffee, meet friends, or find a quiet corner to work. We believe coffee is more than a drink; it's a ritual, a moment of pleasure, and a way to connect with others.
